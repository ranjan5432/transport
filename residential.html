What are the types of house shifting services?

Local residential shifting – Changing your city/town? No worries, while FirstPick is around. With our local packers and movers at your side, we’ve got your household belongings covered and ready to move within your state.

Domestic residential shifting – Get moved safe and sound in your country with our domestic packers and movers at your service. We make sure that your items are secure and intact and get delivered as per the schedule. Our splendid network of packers and movers let you track your things during the transit.

International residential shifting – Got a new home across the territory? International residential shifting is a cumbersome task to perform as it includes a lot of protocols to be followed. Moving athwart borders made simple while FirstPick is around. Our international packers and movers have quite an expertise in this field which makes us one of the leaders in this arena.
